Key Analytical Findings
Q1 — Most dangerous per case?
WRONG_VACCINE and WRONG_DOSE — both 5.0 avg severity. Every single report is a confirmed direct patient safety threat with no dilution.
Q2 — What % of reports are actionable?
Only 9.1% (4,911 of 53,760) map to a specific addressable error. The remaining 89% are OTHER_ERROR — highlighting symptom-text quality limits.
Q3 — Most under-investigated failure?
EXPIRED_VACCINE (2,014 cases). Highest volume specific error yet under-ranked on score — meaning score-only ranking misses a systemic cold chain failure.
Q4 — Is vaccine risk getting worse?
Mixed. Vaccines A and D show clear INCREASING trajectories. Vaccines B and E show DECREASING risk — suggesting effective intervention has occurred.
Q5 — Biggest VSRI driver?
Death (weight 10) dominates. A single fatal event scores equal to 3.3 ER visits or 1.67 hospitalisations — correctly reflecting clinical priority.
Q6 — Specific errors vs total risk burden?
Specific errors generate 27% of total score from only 9.1% of records — they punch far above their frequency weight on severity impact.

VSRI Formula (Expression Transformer)
VSRI Score per Record:

VSRI = (DIED='Y' × 10) + (L_THREAT='Y' × 8) + (HOSPITAL='Y' × 6) + (ER_VISIT='Y' × 4) + (HOSPDAYS>7 × 3)

Minimum score: 0 (no severe outcome)  |  Maximum possible: 31 points
High-Risk flag: Score ≥ 10 triggers HIGH_RISK classification
Null Handling (EXP_PREPROCESS Transformer)
DIED NULL → 'N'  |  HOSPITAL NULL → 'N'  |  ER_VISIT NULL → 'N'  |  L_THREAT NULL → 'N'  |  HOSPDAYS NULL → 0

Data Quality Issues Handled
Problem 1 — NULL severity flags: DIED, HOSPITAL, L_THREAT, ER_VISIT contain NULL when no event occurred.
→ Fix: EXP_PREPROCESS transformer replaces all NULLs with 'N' or 0

Problem 2 — NULL SYMPTOM_TEXT: Records without symptom text cannot be error-classified.
→ Fix: FLT_CLEAN_RECORDS filter gates out records where ISNULL(SYMPTOM_TEXT)

Problem 3 — Unstructured free text: SYMPTOM_TEXT has no schema — must use keyword matching.
→ Fix: INSTR() function in EXP_CLASSIFY searches for priority keywords in priority order
